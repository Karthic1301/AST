Temple / Church Kodimaram

Kodimaram is a flagstaff that is commonly seen in the Hindu temples across South India and Srilanka. It is normally seen in between the Gopuram and the Maha Mandapa (Main hall) of the temple and is usually accompanied by bali peetam and vahana of the concern deity of the temple.
