About Us


Few Words About Us

Durga metal mart based in Nachiyar koil Founded by AR. Sambantham Chettiyar in the year 1974.
He Concentrated mainly in manufacturing of Brass items for Temples like Kuthuvillaku and Pooja articles.
Mr. S. Thiruvalar son of Mr. AR. Sambantham joined in the same business in the year 1977 and started expanding the business to entire Tamilnadu. Also widened the manufacturing range from small Agarbathi stand to Giant size Kuthuvillaku.
More enthusiastic Mr. T. Kathik and Mr. T. Venkatesan, Sons of Mr. Thiruvalar joined in the year 2005. They again concentrated to give better products to their Customers. They also concentrated more in customized items as per the clients need. They also started widening their market internationally.
